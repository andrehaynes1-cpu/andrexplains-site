ABOUT ANDRE

I started my YouTube channel with zero subscribers, zero views, and zero clue what I was doing. No fancy equipment, no connections, no shortcuts. Just a camera and a decision to figure this out.

Andre Explains is where I document that journey in real time. Every tip I share is something I tested on my own channel first. Every mistake I write about is one I actually made. I don't pretend to have all the answers — I share what's working as I figure it out.

This blog and YouTube channel exist for one reason: to help new creators skip the confusion I went through. If you're starting from zero, you're in the right place.
