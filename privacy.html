Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Andre Explains ("we", "our", "the Site") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address and name — when you sign up for our free YouTube Starter Toolkit, request a channel report, or enroll in the YouTube Academy.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, and referral source, collected automatically through Google Analytics.
  • YouTube channel URL — if you request a channel analysis report.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver requested content (reports, toolkits, course access).
  • To send occasional YouTube tips and promotional emails, if you opted in.
  • To understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies to track visitor interactions. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Cookies

The Site uses cookies for analytics purposes (Google Analytics) and to remember user preferences (such as login sessions on the admin panel). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — website usage tracking
  • Supabase — database hosting for blog posts
  • Netlify — website hosting
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — blog post generation (no user data is sent)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with service providers who help operate the Site (as listed above), and only as necessary to provide our services.

7. Email Communications

If you provide your email address, you will receive the content you requested. If you opted in to marketing emails, you may receive occasional tips and offers. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including using HTTPS encryption and secure hosting. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe from email communications at any time.

10. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the Site at andreexplains.com.
